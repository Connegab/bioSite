10 Questions & Answers

  • Q1: What first got you into anime?
    A: My dad introduced me to Attack on Titan when I was younger, and I have been hooked since.
  • Q2: Do you have a favorite anime series right now?
    A: Probably Jujutsu Kaisen. I like the animation and dark story themes.
  • Q3: How often do you play video games?
    A: Almost daily, but it depends on work.
  • Q4: What’s your favorite game you’ve ever played?
    A: Destiny 2, since I put the most hours into it and met a lot of cool people.
  • Q5: What are you currently playing?
    A: Pokémon Go and a mix of single-player games.
  • Q6: What’s the story with World of Warcraft?
    A: Ever since Classic got re-released, I get hooked for about a month each year—it is like tradition now.
  • Q7: Why did you decide to learn Japanese?
    A: My mom is Japanese, and I want to connect more with her side of the family.
  • Q8: Do you find learning Japanese difficult?
    A: Yes, especially kanji, but it is rewarding.
  • Q9: What do you like most about working at Kohl’s?
    A: Being a department lead lets me take responsibility and help coworkers.
  • Q10: Where do you see yourself in five years?
    A: Hopefully fluent in Japanese and working in a job that connects my hobbies with my career.
