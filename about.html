Biography

Sean was born on June 6, 2002, and has grown into someone who can be described as hardworking and passionate as an individual. He is someone whose interests balance creativity, gaming, culture, and leadership. Currently working as a department lead at Kohl’s, Sean demonstrates responsibility and dedication in his professional life while continuing to pursue his personal passions outside of work. One of his favorite places to watch anime is Crunchyroll.

Sean got his love for anime when he was a kid, because his father showed him an anime titled “Attack on Titan,” and he has continued to be fascinated by this form of media ever since. This passion for anime also connects with his personal goal: to learn Japanese in reading, writing, and speaking. His mother’s side of the family lives in Japan, and he views language not only as a personal challenge but also as a bridge to connect more closely with his heritage.

Sean is also an avid gamer, once dedicating countless hours to Destiny 2. Over time, his gaming interests have broadened, making him more of a variety player. Pokémon Go has remained a constant part of his life, encouraging him to explore the world around him, while World of Warcraft Classic brings him back each year with what he calls a tradition at this point, when he immerses himself for a month-long adventure.

Having to balance his work, hobbies, and long-term goals, Sean is definitely someone who takes pride in his responsibilities while he continues to embrace creativity and culture. His story is one of friendship, dedication, and a pursuit of growth both in his personal life and his professional one.
